Our Story

Big flavor, tiny panda energy

Bubble Tea Station Corner is owned and run by two sisters — each pouring their heart into their own neighborhood location. What began as a shared love of authentic, Taiwan-inspired tea drinks has grown into a local favorite across Carson City.

From classic milk teas and Thai tea to rich tiger brown sugar and dreamy color-changing specials, every cup is hand-crafted with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of kawaii spirit — because a good day deserves a great sip.
